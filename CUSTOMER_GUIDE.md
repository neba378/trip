# How to Book Your Trip - SAVANNA Ethiopia Travel

## 🌍 Step-by-Step Booking Guide

### Step 1: Browse Trips
1. Visit the website
2. Click **"Trips"** in the menu
3. Browse available trips
4. Click on a trip you like

### Step 2: View Trip Details
You'll see:
- 📸 Beautiful photos and videos
- 📅 Trip dates
- 💰 Price per person
- 🎁 What's included (food, transport, guide, etc.)
- 📍 Departure location and time

### Step 3: Click "Book Your Spot"
- Big gold button on the trip page
- Opens booking form

### Step 4: Fill Your Information

**Choose Traveler Type:**
- 👤 **Regular** - Normal price
- 🎓 **Student** - 10% discount (bring student ID)
- 👥 **Group 4+** - 10% discount for groups
- 🌍 **Foreigner** - Price in USD

**Enter Details:**
- Full Name (required)
- Phone Number (required) - We'll call you here
- Email (optional)
- Number of People - Use + and - buttons
- Special Requests (optional) - Dietary needs, questions, etc.

**Check Price:**
- See total price at bottom
- Discounts applied automatically

Click **"Continue to Payment"**

### Step 5: Make Payment FIRST

**IMPORTANT: You must make the payment BEFORE uploading screenshot!**

**You'll see payment accounts:**
- **Telebirr**: 0944 780 840
- **CBE**: 1000524300657
- **Abyssinia**: 67531167

**How to Pay:**

#### Option A: Telebirr
1. Open Telebirr app
2. Send money to: **0944 780 840**
3. Amount: [Your total from website]
4. **Wait for "Transaction Successful" message**
5. Take screenshot of the success message

#### Option B: CBE Birr
1. Open CBE Birr app
2. Send to: **1000524300657**
3. Amount: [Your total]
4. **Wait for confirmation**
5. Screenshot the confirmation

#### Option C: Bank Transfer
1. Go to bank or use mobile banking
2. Transfer to account: **67531167** (Abyssinia)
3. **Complete the transfer**
4. Get receipt or screenshot

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- ✅ **DO** make payment first
- ✅ **DO** wait for success confirmation
- ✅ **DO** take screenshot showing success
- ❌ **DON'T** upload screenshot before paying
- ❌ **DON'T** use old screenshots
- ❌ **DON'T** edit or modify screenshots

### Step 6: Upload Payment Screenshot (AFTER Payment)

**Important:**
- Screenshot must be **clear and readable**
- Must show:
  - ✅ Amount you paid
  - ✅ Date of payment
  - ✅ Transaction successful
- Click **"Click to upload screenshot"**
- Select your screenshot photo
- You'll see preview

Click **"Continue"**

### Step 7: Confirm Booking

**Review everything:**
- Your name and phone
- Trip details
- Total amount
- Payment screenshot

**If correct:**
- Click **"Submit Booking"**

**If wrong:**
- Click **"Back"** to fix

### Step 8: Get Confirmation Code

🎉 **Success!**

You'll see:
- ✅ Green checkmark
- Your confirmation code (Example: **SAV-ABC123**)

**IMPORTANT:**
- 📸 **Screenshot this code!**
- 💾 **Save it safely**
- You'll need it for your trip

### Step 9: Wait for Confirmation Call

**What happens next:**
- ⏰ Within 24 hours, SAVANNA team will:
  - Check your payment screenshot
  - Verify the payment
  - Call you to confirm

**When they call:**
- They'll confirm your booking
- Give you final details
- Answer any questions

## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: When will I get confirmed?
**A:** Within 24 hours after you submit your booking.

### Q: What if my payment screenshot is rejected?
**A:** We'll call you to explain. You can resend a correct screenshot.

### Q: Can I cancel my booking?
**A:** Call us at 0944 780 840 to discuss cancellation.

### Q: What should I bring on the trip?
**A:** We'll tell you when we confirm your booking. Usually:
- ID card
- Comfortable clothes
- Water bottle
- Camera

### Q: Where do we meet?
**A:** Most trips depart from **Mexico Square, Addis Ababa**. Exact location and time will be confirmed when we call you.

### Q: Is food included?
**A:** Yes! Check your trip details - most include full meals.

### Q: Can I pay cash?
**A:** No, please pay via Telebirr, CBE, or bank transfer and upload screenshot.

### Q: I don't have a smartphone to upload screenshot
**A:** Call us at 0944 780 840 and we'll help you book over the phone.
